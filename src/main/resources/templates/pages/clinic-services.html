PREVENTION - VACCINATIONS

We can build protection against various infectious diseases according to a scheme with properly selected vaccination preparations. Regular vaccination protects you, your pet and others from contagious diseases. Initial vaccinations build a protective wall (immunity), and annual revaccinations maintain it at the level necessary to protect the body. Make an appointment to review and determine the most appropriate vaccination schedule.

PREVENTION - DEWORMING

Regular external and internal deworming and ridding the body of ticks, fleas, intestinal, lung and heartworms and others is extremely important for the good maintenance of your pet's health. Deworming is according to a scheme according to the individual indicators and needs of your pet. Make an appointment to review and determine the most appropriate deworming program.

MICROCHIPS

The procedure of placing a chip on your pet should be carried out by a specialist. It's a one-time thing and can save you a lot of headaches when your pet runs away, gets lost in the park, gets stolen, etc. The chip contains information about your pet and about you as the owner, which is easily read by special readers for this purpose. If you are still wondering and haven't done it yet, make an appointment for a consultation on our phones.

GROOMING SALOON

For the beauty and health of your pet's skin and hair, we offer grooming for dogs with short, medium and long hair. When grooming, we are always guided by the established standards for the respective breed.

In the process of work, we do not use narcotic drugs. We use high-quality and hypoallergenic cosmetics. All tools and special equipment are cleaned, disinfected and sterilized daily. After grooming, we perform standard bathing, drying, nail trimming and ear cleaning. Rely on the care of our groomers, who follow global trends and constantly upgrade their skills at special courses.

Pre-registration for day and time is required.

HOTEL

If you are away from home and your pet cannot travel with you, our hotel is at your disposal. Its capacity is up to 10 small dogs or 3 large and 4 small, separately up to 6 cats.

Soon we will have separate spaces for parrots, rabbits, hamsters and fish. For four-legged pets, we have a yard for free movement and walking.

For each animal, there is a separate protected rest area with an individual bed and bowls for food and water. The price is for a 24-hour stay with food included. If the animal is on a special diet, you can bring its preferred food.

If he needs special care and services for the days of stay, we will try to provide them.
