CLOSE TO THE SCHOOL

Introducing children to the profession of veterinary doctor. We conduct open lessons after pre-registration and arrangement with the class head and permission received from the principal of the relevant school. The subject is dedicated to our pets, how to care for them, why they need to be feed with certain foods, how to respond in emergencies, simulation of a small surgery. The played situation is very fun for kids. See the gallery of photos and the happiness in their eyes.

PHARMACY

We are planning to open a Pharmacy to close the loop regarding the treatment of your pets. In this way, we will help our customers in their search and ensure that the prescribed medication of the treatment will be available at any time and place.
