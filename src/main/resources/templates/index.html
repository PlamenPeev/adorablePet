ABOUT US

We are a small community of veterinarians, technicians, lab technicians, trainers, groomers and receptionists united in the common idea of offering people a variety of services to help their beloved pets. The clinic was established in 2017 and we are growing gradually by expanding our activities.

Veterinarians and specialists are available at the clinic to help you diagnose health problems and prescribe the correct treatment for recovery.

We have modern equipment for imaging diagnostics and a laboratory. We work with the latest technology in our operating rooms.

We offer premium services with our internationally recognized groomers who will give you advice according to your pet and how to care, maintain their hygiene, and what they need depending on the season. Our hotel part has comfortable separate areas to ensure the necessary comfort of our friends. And our trainers will help you fight bad behavioral reactions.
